Step dots: what the done state becomes

The run keeps the step-dots progress you picked (filled = done, pulsing = current). The question is the landing: what the three dots turn into when the run finishes. Four answers below, each auto plays. The line stays tappable for the breakdown, during the run and after.

Option A

Merge into one status light

Searching 0.0s

The side dots slide into the middle one, which gives a little pop and stays as a single green light: "● Curated in 8.4s". Reads like a status LED, matches the live badge language in the topbar.

Option B

Morph into a check

Searching 0.0s

The dots shrink away and a small check springs in where they were: "✓ Curated in 8.4s". The most conventional "task finished" signal, slightly louder than A.

Option C

Dissolve, text only

Searching 0.0s

The dots dissolve and the text slides over: just "Curated in 8.4s" in muted mono, like message metadata. The quietest option, the loader stops asking for attention the moment the posts land.

Option D

Dissolve, outcome counts up

Searching 0.0s

Dots dissolve and the line becomes the result, with the number counting up: "25 posts in 8.4s". The payoff is the outcome itself rather than a glyph, and it doubles as the post count readout.
